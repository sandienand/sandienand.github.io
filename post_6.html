My GRC Career Sprint, Take 2. The Adaptive Plan for Leadership

Published: October 2025 by The Integrity Protocol

Hi, all. Following last week's update, I'm excited to share a deeper dive into the 'how' of my pivot into Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) leadership. This isn't a sudden career change; it's a strategic evolution.

My years in financial services, mastering AML/KYC compliance, process optimisation, and large-scale team leadership, have given me a robust foundation in the 'G' and 'R' of GRC. Now, I'm focused on acquiring the rigorous technical understanding required for modern cyber-centric risk.

As someone with ADHD, I know a rigid plan is often a recipe for burnout. My approach is designed to be constructively adaptable, ensuring curiosity fuels my momentum without derailing my ultimate objective. This plan is less about a fixed timeline and more about continuous learning and application in the GRC field.

Key Pillars of the Adaptive Plan

This phase is built on three core pillars: Technical Fluency (Python/Cyber Security), Certification (CISSP), and Practical Application (Project Portfolio). Each element supports the others, creating a holistic foundation for GRC leadership.
